The Brnk Community FAF Mod Collection

A collecion of mods used to improve our FAF playing experience. More pages are under development and will be available soon.

Phantom Balance

Adjusts the FAF balance to compensate better for Phantom-X games.

4 Phantoms

Allows for 4 phantoms. Recommended for games with 11 players or more. Also fixes some scoreboard issues.

Simspeed++

Mitigates late-game simulation speed slowdown by introducing performance optimisations and reduced unit particle effects. Includes features for Phantom-X.

Balance++

Adjusts the FAF balance to offset the official patch's bias towards high-ranked 1v1s. Makes the T2 stage more viable.

Contribute

If you have any ideas, feel free to talk to either Rowey or Azroc, the mod developers, about your idea.
